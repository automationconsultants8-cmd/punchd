The Problem

Time theft costs thousands.

Construction companies lose an average of 7% of payroll to time theft every year.

Buddy Punching

Workers clock in for absent coworkers. Without verification, there's no way to know who's actually there.

Time Rounding

Early clock-ins and late clock-outs add up. Those extra minutes become hours of lost money weekly.

Ghost Entries

Without GPS, workers can clock in from anywhere. No confirmation they're actually at the job site.

About

Why I built Punch'd

I grew up around people who worked hard for their paychecks. I saw what happened when time got rounded wrong, when someone's hours got shorted, when disputes turned into arguments. It wasn't right.

On the flip side, I also saw business owners getting burned by workers who weren't being honest. That wasn't right either.

So I built Punch'd to solve both problems. Workers clock in, their face is verified, their location is confirmed, and everyone knows exactly what happened. No disputes. No guessing. Just the truth.

Integrity has always been important to me. This is my way of bringing it to the workforce.

Jude Smith Founder
